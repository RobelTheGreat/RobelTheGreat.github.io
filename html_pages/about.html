Our plans as a club

Our club is a club that is located in the UK. We very much specialize in walking because of the benefits that it brings to people in our society. As members of the club we aim to gather as many people as we can from different cultures to mix and mingle, and to also create an activity that helps people bond and relax. To execute our aim as a club, we have reopened our different walks. So as a club we hope you join us to go on this wonderful journey.
